spacy usage

发表于 2018-10-16 | 分类于 spacy

与许多NLP库一样，spaCy将所有字符串编码为哈希值，以减少内存使用并提高效率。 因此，为了获得可读字符串的属性表示，我们需要在其名称中添加下划线_：

interpretion

text: The original word text.
lemma_: The base form of the word.(基准词)
pos_: The simple part-of-speech tag.(简单词性标签)
tag_: The detailed part-of-speech tag.(复杂词性标签)
dep_: Syntactic dependency, i.e. the relation between tokens.(语法依赖，即标记之间的关系)
shape_: The word shape – capitalisation, punctuation, digits.(单词形状 - 大写，标点符号，数字)
is_alpha: Is the token an alpha character?(是字母吗?)
is_stop: Is the token part of a stop list, i.e. the most common words of the language?(是否停止词)
